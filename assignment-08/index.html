How To Tie Your Shoes Correctly

Step 1.

Indicate your right and left lace

Step 2.

Cross the left lace over the right lace

Step 3.

Take the right lace (which is now on your left) and tuck it through the open hole

Step 4.

Now make 2 loops or bunny ears with the ends of the laces

Step 5.

Cross your left bunny ear over your right bunny ear

Step 6.

Take the right bunny ear (which is now on your left) and tuck it through the open hole

Step 7.

Tighten to comcort by pulling on both ends of your bunny ears

Step 8.

You can double knot it by repeating Step 7
