Ingredients

  1. Eggs
  2. Oil
  3. Salt
  4. Pepper

Directions

  1. Heat pan with small drizzle of oil
  2. Once the pan is hot add prefered amount of eggs
  3. let eggs cook for 1min
  4. Add 1/2 tsp of water in pan and cover the pan with lid
  5. Leave the lid on for longer to have a harder or softer yoke
  6. Once eggs are cooked place them on to a plate
  7. Now add desired amount of salt and pepper
  8. Congrats you have made the best sunny side eggs ever becasue you made them!!
